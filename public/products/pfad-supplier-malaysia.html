What is PFAD?

Palm Fatty Acid Distillate is a co-product of the physical refining of crude palm oil. When crude palm oil is deodorised, the free fatty acids are stripped off as a distillate — that distillate is PFAD. It is a high-FFA, semi-solid material at room temperature, valued as a feedstock precisely because it is a residue stream rather than a food-grade oil. This makes it attractive both for biodiesel production and for the oleochemical sector, where it is converted into soaps, candles, fatty acids and other downstream products.

Applications

  • Biodiesel feedstock. PFAD's high free-fatty-acid content suits esterification routes and is widely processed into FAME.
  • Oleochemicals. A base material for fatty acids, soap noodles, stearin and downstream derivatives.
  • Renewable diesel (HVO). Used by refiners able to process high-FFA streams within their pre-treatment envelope.

Sourcing and quality control

We source PFAD from MPOB-licensed refiners and check every consignment before loading, issuing a certificate of analysis per batch. Because PFAD is temperature-sensitive and solidifies, we coordinate heated ISO-tank or bulk-vessel logistics so the cargo arrives in a discharge-ready condition. Note that PFAD's sustainability classification differs from waste-based feedstocks such as UCO or POME — we provide the documentation available for the material and are transparent about its status so your compliance team can classify it correctly.

Frequently asked questions

What is the typical FFA of your PFAD?

PFAD typically runs above 70% free fatty acid (as palmitic), with total fatty matter above 95%. Exact values are reported on the per-batch certificate of analysis.

How is PFAD shipped given that it solidifies?

PFAD is a semi-solid at ambient temperature, so we coordinate heated ISO-tank or bulk-vessel logistics to ensure the cargo arrives in a discharge-ready condition.

Is PFAD classified as a waste-based feedstock?

PFAD's sustainability classification differs from waste streams such as UCO or POME. We are transparent about its status and provide the documentation available for the material so your compliance team can classify it correctly.

Can you supply PFAD on a recurring contract?

Yes. We serve both spot parcels and recurring monthly programmes, and welcome long-term offtake discussions that let us plan volume and hold grade.
