Used Cooking Oil (UCO) Supplier & Exporter in Malaysia

Consistent-grade, traceable Used Cooking Oil for biodiesel, renewable diesel and oleochemical producers — exported FOB and CIF from Malaysian ports with a certificate of analysis on every batch.

SafOil is a Malaysia-based Used Cooking Oil (UCO) supplier and exporter, sourcing filtered waste cooking oil from a vetted network of collectors, restaurants and food manufacturers across the country. We supply refiners producing biodiesel (FAME), renewable diesel (HVO) and sustainable aviation fuel (SAF), delivering consistent grades with the documentation international buyers need for compliance reporting.

Used Cooking Oil is one of the most valuable waste-based feedstocks in the renewable-fuels market because it carries a low carbon intensity and, in most jurisdictions, qualifies for advanced or waste-based incentive treatment. But the market only works when the material arriving at the refinery matches the material that was contracted. That is the problem SafOil is built to solve: the spec you contract is the spec you receive, shipment after shipment.

Why refiners buy UCO from SafOil

Procurement teams at biodiesel and renewable-diesel plants evaluate feedstock suppliers on three things — and we are built around all three.

  • Consistency. Standardised filtering and per-batch testing mean grade drift between shipments is controlled, not left to chance.
  • Certification-ready supply. ISCC-aligned, RED II–compatible documentation with full chain of custody, so the volume counts toward your sustainability targets and mass-balance reporting.
  • Clean logistics. Flexitank, ISO tank or bulk arrangements, FOB or CIF to your discharge port, with a dedicated trade desk handling documentation end to end.

Traceability and chain of custody

Every consignment is lab-tested before loading and ships with a certificate of analysis and sustainability documentation. Because UCO's value in the EU and US depends on its waste-based status, we maintain chain-of-custody records from the collection network through to export — the paperwork that proves the material is what the label says it is. This is what lets a European or North American buyer report the volume with confidence during an audit.

Export markets

SafOil ships UCO to biodiesel and HVO refiners in the European Union, where RED II and RED III targets drive demand for waste-based feedstock; to North America, where renewable diesel and SAF producers operate under LCFS and RFS incentive programmes; and to regional buyers across East and South Asia seeking steady, traceable supply. We quote FOB Malaysian ports or CIF to your nominated port.

Frequently asked questions

What FFA grade of used cooking oil does SafOil supply?

We supply standard UCO at roughly 3–5% FFA, with low-FFA premium grades available on request. Every shipment carries a batch certificate of analysis reporting the actual FFA, moisture and impurity values, so your intake team knows the exact grade before discharge.

Do you provide ISCC or chain-of-custody documentation for UCO?

Yes. We maintain ISCC-aligned, RED II–compatible documentation with chain-of-custody records from the collection network through export, so European and North American buyers can report the volume as waste-based feedstock during audits.

What are your minimum order volumes and packing options?

UCO ships in flexitanks of around 24 MT or in ISO tanks. We handle both spot cargoes and recurring monthly programmes; share your target monthly volume and we will structure an offer that fits.

Do you quote FOB or CIF?

Both. We quote FOB Malaysian ports or CIF to your nominated discharge port, with the trade desk managing documentation and shipping end to end.
