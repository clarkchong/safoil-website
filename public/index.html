About Us

A dedicated biofuel feedstock specialist.

SafOil is a Malaysia-based supplier and exporter of waste-based and residual oil feedstock for the global biofuel industry.

We source, process and export a full range of feedstocks — Used Cooking Oil, tallow, fish oil, PFAD, POME, EFB oil, spent bleaching earth oil and vegetable acid oils — supplying biodiesel, renewable diesel and sustainable aviation fuel producers across Europe, North America and Asia.

Built around the principles of consistency, compliance and clean documentation, SafOil combines disciplined trade execution with ISCC-aligned traceability to give refiners a feedstock partner they can rely on — shipment after shipment.

Product Range

Waste & residue feedstock, ready to ship.

SafOil supplies a full range of waste-based and residual oil feedstocks for biodiesel, renewable diesel (HVO) and SAF producers — from used cooking oil and animal fats to palm residues and vegetable acid oils. Every consignment is lab-tested before loading, with a full certificate of analysis per batch and ISCC-aligned traceability.

How It Works

From supplier to certified cargo.

A transparent, four-stage chain that keeps your feedstock compliant and your documentation audit-ready.

01

Source

Feedstock is sourced from a vetted network of trusted suppliers and processors across Malaysia and the region.

02

Quality-check

Material is checked and, where needed, processed to meet target FFA and moisture grades before dispatch.

03

Verify

Independent lab COA per batch, with full chain-of-custody and sustainability documentation.

04

Export

Loaded in flexitanks, ISO tanks or bulk vessels — FOB or CIF to your port of choice.
