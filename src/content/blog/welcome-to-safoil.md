Welcome — and thank you for stopping by. If you have found us through LinkedIn, this post is the short version of who we are and why we started SafOil.

## The gap we set out to fill

The waste-based feedstock trade runs on trust, but it doesn't always run on documentation. Buyers we spoke to kept describing the same frustration: material that arrived off-spec, paperwork that didn't hold up at audit, and suppliers who went quiet the moment a shipment got complicated. Meanwhile, demand for used cooking oil, palm residues and acid oils keeps climbing as biodiesel, renewable diesel and SAF mandates tighten around the world.

SafOil exists to close that gap — to be the supplier whose contracted spec matches the delivered spec, whose chain-of-custody documentation stands up to scrutiny, and whose trade desk actually answers.

## What we supply

We source, grade and export a full range of waste-based and residual oil feedstock from Malaysia and the region:

- **Used Cooking Oil (UCO)** — filtered, standard and low-FFA premium grades
- **PFAD** — high-FFA palm refining co-product, bulk export volumes
- **POME and EFB oil** — Annex IX advanced palm residues, double-counting eligible under RED II
- **SBEO and vegetable acid oils** — soya, cottonseed and rice bran acid oils
- **Tallow and fish oil** — animal-fat and marine lipid feedstock

Every consignment is lab-tested before loading and ships with a certificate of analysis, FOB Malaysian ports or CIF to your discharge port.

## How we work

Three principles guide every deal: **consistency** — the spec you contract is the spec you receive; **certification-ready supply** — ISCC-aligned, RED II–compatible documentation with full chain of custody; and **responsiveness** — a real trade desk that replies within three working days, spot or contract.
